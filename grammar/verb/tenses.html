Verb tenses

There are three tenses:

  1. Present
  2. Past
  3. Future

and four aspects:

  1. Simple / Indefinite
  2. Continious / Progressive
  3. Perfect
  4. Perfect Continious / Perfect Progressive

Sentences structure

Affirmative sentence

Subject + modal + Verb

Negative sentence

Subject + modal + not + Verb

Interrogative sentence

modal + Subject + Verb
